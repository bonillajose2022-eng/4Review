📋 Sequencing Words Palabras de Secuencia

What are sequencing words? / ¿Qué son las palabras de secuencia?

Sequencing words help us tell the order of events — like steps in a recipe or a story.

Las palabras de secuencia nos ayudan a contar el orden de los eventos — como pasos en una receta o una historia.

1st

FIRST

Primero

Use First to start.

🌟 First, wash your hands.
2nd

NEXT

Después/Luego

Use Next for step 2.

🌟 Next, open your book.
3rd

THEN

Entonces/Luego

Use Then to continue.

🌟 Then, read the page.
4th

AFTER THAT

Después de eso

Use After that for a later step.

🌟 After that, answer the questions.
Last

FINALLY

Finalmente

Use Finally to end.

🌟 Finally, close your book.

📖 Example: How to make a sandwich / Cómo hacer un sándwich

  1. First, get the bread.
  2. Next, put butter on the bread.
  3. Then, add cheese and ham.
  4. After that, put the other slice on top.
  5. Finally, enjoy your sandwich!

💡 Remember! / ¡Recuerda!

Always use a comma (,) after sequencing words at the beginning of a sentence!

¡Siempre usa una coma (,) después de estas palabras al inicio de la oración!

Example: First, I wake up early.

🔗 Connectives Conectivos

What are connectives? / ¿Qué son los conectivos?

Connectives join two ideas or sentences together. They make our writing flow better!

Los conectivos unen dos ideas u oraciones. ¡Hacen que nuestra escritura fluya mejor!

➕

AND

Y

Use and to add information. / Úsalo para agregar información.

I like cats and dogs.
Me gustan los gatos y los perros.
She sings and dances.
Ella canta y baila.

↔️

BUT

Pero

Use but to show a contrast. / Úsalo para mostrar un contraste.

I like pizza, but I don't like pasta.
Me gusta la pizza, pero no me gusta la pasta.
He is tall, but his brother is short.
Él es alto, pero su hermano es bajo.

❓

BECAUSE

Porque

Use because to give a reason. / Úsalo para dar una razón.

I study hard because I want good grades.
Estudio mucho porque quiero buenas notas.
She is happy because it's her birthday.
Ella está feliz porque es su cumpleaños.

Quick Reference / Referencia Rápida

WordEspañolFunctionExample
ANDYAdds informationI read and write every day.
BUTPeroShows contrastI like sports, but I hate running.
BECAUSEPorqueGives a reasonI smile because I am happy.

📚 Irregular Verbs: BE & HAVE Verbos Irregulares

Regular vs. Irregular Verbs

✅ Regular Verbs

Add -ed to make past tense. / Agrega -ed para el pasado.

  • play → played
  • walk → walked
  • talk → talked

⚡ Irregular Verbs

They change completely in the past! / ¡Cambian completamente!

  • go → went
  • see → saw
  • be → was/were

The verb "BE" – Ser / Estar

PersonPresentPastExample
IamwasI am happy. / I was happy yesterday.
YouarewereYou are kind. / You were kind.
He/She/ItiswasShe is smart. / She was smart.
WearewereWe are friends. / We were friends.
TheyarewereThey are here. / They were here.
Base: be
Past: was / were
Past Participle: been

The verb "HAVE" – Tener

PersonPresentPastExample
IhavehadI have a dog. / I had a dog.
YouhavehadYou have a book. / You had a book.
He/She/IthashadHe has a bike. / He had a bike.
WehavehadWe have fun. / We had fun.
TheyhavehadThey have cats. / They had cats.
Base: have
Past: had
Past Participle: had

📖 Reading Activities Actividades de Lectura

🟢 Easy

🐕 My Dog Max

My name is Ana. I have a dog. His name is Max. Max is big and brown.

First, I wake up and feed Max. Then, I take him for a walk. After that, I play with him in the yard. Finally, I give Max a bath.

Max is happy because he loves to play. I love Max!

📝 Comprehension Questions:

📖 Vocabulary:

dog – perro
big – grande
walk – paseo
yard – patio
bath – baño
happy – feliz
🟡 Medium

🌱 The School Garden

Our class has a garden at school. We are very excited!

First, we prepared the soil and dug small holes. Next, we put seeds in the holes. Then, we watered the plants every day. After that, we watched plants grow. Finally, we had fresh vegetables!

We were happy because our hard work paid off. Our teacher said we are great gardeners!

📝 Comprehension Questions:

📖 Vocabulary:

garden – jardín
excited – emocionado
soil – tierra
seeds – semillas
watered – regó
vegetables – verduras
🔴 Hard

🏔️ The Mountain Adventure

Last summer, my family and I had an incredible adventure in the mountains. First, we packed our bags because the weather could change quickly.

Next, we drove for three hours. Then, we hiked through beautiful forests. After that, we were amazed by the view.

Finally, we had a picnic and took photos. My legs were tired but my heart was full. It was the best day of my life!

📝 Comprehension Questions:

📖 Vocabulary:

incredible – increíble
packed – empacó
trail – sendero
hike – caminata
amazed – asombrado
picnic – picnic
